The History of Gilligan

Who is he?

Gilligan is my cat. He is an orange tabby cat that currently resides with my parents in New Hampshire. He is very grumpy, and does not like people very much. The only two people he likes are my parents. He likes to go outside while supervised, but he will not walk on grass.

Backstory

Gilligan was born on April 23rd, 2013. We got him in July 2013. We adopted him from a shelter in Salem, New Hampshire. He had 2 siblings at the shelter. Gilligan was named at the shelter in a charity auction to raise funds. His kitty family all has names from the show "Gilligan's Island". I decided to keep his name because I thought it was creative.
